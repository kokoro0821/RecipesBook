Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cups all-purpose flour
  • Tsp baking powder
  • Tsp baking soda
  • 1pinch Salt
  • Cup Sugar
  • Cup oil
  • 1cup milk
  • 1Tbsp vinegar
  • 2Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 100grams chocolate

Step 1

Perparation for cooker recipe.

Step 2

Spread salt in the pressure cooker.

Step 3

Put on the plate on Salt to cover.

Step 4

Remove the gasket or ring and waight.

Step 5

Cover the cooker.Heat it on a medium flame.

Step 6

Add suger and milk in the cooker

Step 7

Pour melted butter or oil. If you use oil use coconut oil.

Step 8

Add vanilla extract and lemon juice or vinegar.

Step 9

stir until the suger dissolves.

Step 10

Place a sieve over the bowl.Add flour, salt baking powder & baking soda. mix them up lightly.

Step 11

Sieve it.

Step 12

Gently mix until just combined. Do not over mix.

Step 13

Transfar the batter to the cake tray. Tap the cake tray few times.

Step 14

Place the cake tray inside.

Cover the cooker. bake 50~60 mins.

Step 16

Make a litle holes. than bake mins.

Step 17

Cool the cake on a ware rack for 10~15 mins

Step 18

Use a litle chocolate icing. Heated 2cups of water with kadai.

Step 19

stir continously until the chocolate melt and smooth.

Step 20

Cool the cake and chocolate completely. pour the chocolate over the cake. spread it with a supatula.
